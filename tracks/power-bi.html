Build notes
  • Model: WideWorldImportersDW (Fact.Sale, Fact.Stock Holding, Dimension.Date, Customer, Stock Item).
  • Measures used: Total Sales, Total Quantity, YoY Sales %, comparisons (e.g., Avg/Max across items).

Build Notes

  • Data model: star schema with Date, Region, and Metric dimensions.
  • DAX: YTD, rolling 12-month, and % change vs prior year.
  • Design: consistent color palette, KPI cards, and small multiples.
